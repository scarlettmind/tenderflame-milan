Halo

Inspired by primordial cave civilizations. The Halo's mirror-polished stainless steel cave-form reflects both flame and ambient light in an endless, meditative loop — fire as architecture, warmth as wonder. Presented first at Ambiente Frankfurt, then unveiled in its full scale in Milan.

Torch

Dancing fire. The Torch distils millennia of ceremonial flame into a single controlled object — vertical, sovereign, alive. Tenderflame's steel-wick burns clean and smoke-free, yet nothing about its presence feels tamed.
